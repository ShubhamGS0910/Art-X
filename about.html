About Jagdamb

A grounded, verification-first partner for industrial land, vendors and project delivery.

Our Story

Built from the ground up — literally

Jagdamb Sourcing Solutions began in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad) with a simple observation: industries entering Maharashtra's growing corridors were spending months chasing land records, vendor references and approval paperwork separately, often through unreliable intermediaries.

We built Jagdamb to close that gap — combining on-ground survey work, vendor verification and project coordination into a single, transparent process. What started as land sourcing in Bidkin and Shendra has grown into a full industrial facilitation practice covering vendor sourcing, factory setup and project management.
